IPSEA (the Independent Provider of Special Education Advice) offers free, legally accurate advice for families navigating the SEND system in England. Their website includes template letters for every stage of the EHCP process, factsheets written in plain English, and a tribunal support service.

If you are in the middle of an EHCP assessment, preparing for an annual review, or considering an appeal, this is the first place to go. Their resources are written by people who understand both the law and the reality of what families are dealing with.
